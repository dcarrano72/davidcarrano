About me

I am an artist and web developer with many years of experience with illustration and using graphics software and code editors to create digital art and code for interactive web graphics and user interface design.

My interest in art and technology started early. I started drawing as a kid and exceled. When I was very young I got my first computer. It was a TRS 80 Mc-10 running BASIC on ROM. I followed the instructions to program my first dice game and I was hooked. Around the same time, I got my first airbrush and started painting t-shirts. I loved how fast you I could represent shading and depth. I began doing drawings and painting projects for people here and there.

I continued doing freelance art projects here and there over the years while working other jobs. I got hired at a Broadway scenic studio where I learned backdrop painting

In 1998 I started working for an internet company doing digital image editing. Then I bought my first graphics computer system. There I started experimenting with creating animations in Flash. I began learning action script to create interactivity for my animations. To display my flash projects, I started building html pages and embedding flash files. I continued building websites for myself and occasionally for small businesses and in 2009 I started building websites full time. In 2019 I graduated from General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive. Now I am a freelance web developer living in Shelton CT. where I study programming and build websites for small businesses and personal projects.
